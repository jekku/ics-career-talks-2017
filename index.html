TIP #2

Understand interviews and jobs

ABSOLUTELY DO NOT:


  • Tell a lie on your resume
  • Be late for interviews
  • Leave without asking questions
  • Do a take home exam that seems too unique or exorbitant

Probably the right thing to do:


  • Keep your resume truthful and brief
  • Arrive early during interviews
  • Ask questions when given the chance

TIP #3

Learn how to read job offers and contracts

Some red lights or things to watch out for:


  • No inclusion of mandated government benefits
  • Relatively long contract bonds
  • Unregistered businesses
  • Unspecified length of probation period
  • Statements that can be put against you
  • Does not align with your long term goals

Go signals for consideration:


  • Inverse of red lights previously specified
  • Richness in people, exposure, and tech
  • Aligns with your goals for the next 5 or more years
